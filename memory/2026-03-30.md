# 2026-03-30 — Instagram Model Credits Crisis & Gemini Re-Classification

## 🔴 Critical Discovery: All Model Credits Were Wrong

**Problem discovered:** 04:20 CET  
Lothar manually checked 12 posts (6 just published + 6 from previous days):
- **Result: 12/12 handles were WRONG**
- Either handles didn't exist on Instagram, or were completely different people
- This had been happening for DAYS without detection

**Root cause analysis:**
1. LLaVA 7B handle extraction was unreliable (invented/guessed handles)
2. Content image → Screenshot matching logic was fundamentally broken
3. No visual verification step to confirm handle = person in photo
4. 97% verification (yesterday) only checked if handles existed, NOT if they matched the photos

**Impact:**
- 6 posts published today (04:58) with wrong credits — Lothar deleted them manually
- Unknown number of posts from previous days also had wrong credits
- Serious copyright compliance issue

## Decision: Complete Process Overhaul

**Old broken approach:**
- LLaVA 7B extracts handles from screenshots
- Fuzzy matching links content images to screenshots by filename proximity
- No verification that person in photo = Instagram handle

**New approach (approved by Lothar 05:37):**
1. Gemini 2.5 Flash classifies each image: "screenshot" or "clean_image"
2. Pairing rule: Each screenshot + all clean_images directly after it (until next screenshot) = one group
3. Extract handle from each screenshot
4. Output: `{"handle": "@name", "images": ["file1.jpg", "file2.jpg"]}`
5. Manual spot-check before continuing
